You became a lawyer.
Not an admin.

But somewhere between the intake emails, the document reminders, the billing follow-ups, and the "just checking in" messages — that's exactly what happened. The good news: every one of those tasks is something an agent can own. Here's where it starts.

01 — THE LEAK

Leads Going Cold Before Anyone Responds

Potential clients contact you and hear nothing for hours. They've already moved on. An intake agent responds in under 60 seconds, qualifies them, and books the consult — automatically, around the clock.

02 — THE DRAG

Chasing the Same Documents. Again.

Your paralegal spends hours every week sending the same follow-up emails. An agent takes over the entire sequence — Day 3, Day 8, Day 14 — and only escalates when it's actually earned your attention.

03 — THE DRAIN

Invoices Sitting Unpaid Because Nobody Followed Up

Billing reminders require consistency you can't always give. A billing agent delivers invoices, sends email and SMS reminders on schedule, and escalates overdue accounts — every cycle, without fail.
